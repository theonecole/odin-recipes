Yummy Burgers!

This is my recipe for delicious, yummy, burgers! Please take a look!

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns (brioche)
  • Ground Beef
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Red Onion
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Mayonaise

Steps

  1. Slice and chop vegetables into preferred portions
  2. Take your desired beef amount and form a ball
  3. Place beef ball in a pan over medium head, using a spatula press on the ball to flatten and form the burger
  4. Cook for 4 Minutes then flip to other side
  5. Once flipped place 1 slice of cheese on burger and cook for another 4 minutes
  6. During this, toast bun if desired in toaster, once toasted spread mayo on bun
  7. After 4 minutes have passed place burger on the bun, place your vegetables on top of the cheese, and enjoy!
