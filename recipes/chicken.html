Chicken Burgers

These are Chicken Burgers that use cornflakes as breading. Lighter and yummy!

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • Chicken Breast
  • Mayo
  • Sriracha
  • Sliced Pickles
  • Cornflakes
  • Seasonings
  • Egg White

Steps

  1. Crush desired cornflakes and mix with Seasonings of choice
  2. Dip and coat chicken breast in egg white and then press onto cornflakes and make it covered
  3. Preheat oven to 400 degrees farenheight
  4. Place chicken on baking sheet in oven for 20 minutes, flip halfway
  5. Toast Bun in toaster
  6. Mix mayo and Sriracha for hot sauce
  7. Once burger is done, assemble with bun, pickle, sauce, and burger, thanks!
