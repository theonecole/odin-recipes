Greek Yogurt Pizza Dough Pizza

This pizza is made with greek yogurt in it's dough! Give it a try!

Ingredients

  • Greek Yogurt Plain
  • Flour
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Turkey Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Mix Greek yogurt and flour in a 1:1 ratio for your desired amount of dough
  2. Once dough has reached dought status, form pizza dough shape on a pizza pan or baking sheet
  3. Place desired pizza sauce on dough
  4. Cover in cheese, and place turkey Pepperonis on top
  5. Preheat oven to 400 degrees farenheight
  6. Put pizza in oven for 15 minutes and check on it every few minutes
  7. Once desired look of pizza is complete, take out of oven and enjoy!
